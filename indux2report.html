Engineering Thinking - R&D at wind sector by Yanni Zhong - Advisory Engineer, Siemens Gamesa

Event Report

The IEEE Industry Applications Society Student Branch Chapter College of Engineering Karunagappally organized the second talk of the Indux webinar series on the 17th of April 2023 from 7.00 PM IST. The talk was on the topic 'Engineering Thinking - R&D at wind Sector' and was handled by Ms. Yanni Zhong, Advisory Engineer, Seimens Gamesa. The session on Engineering Thinking - R&D in the Wind Sector provided valuable insights into the research and development efforts in the field of wind energy. The session highlighted the significance of engineering thinking in driving innovation, improving efficiency, and addressing the challenges faced by the wind industry. The session began with some basic quizes of windmill with which Ms. Yanni succeeded in catching the attention of the participants. The session discussed advancements in materials and manufacturing techniques for wind turbine components. The session addressed the importance of environmental considerations and sustainability in wind energy development. The Engineering Thinking - R&D in the Wind Sector session provided valuable insights into the ongoing research and development efforts in the wind industry. It underscored the importance of engineering thinking and collaboration in addressing the challenges and maximizing the opportunities in the field. The session highlighted the need for continued investment in R&D to drive innovation, improve performance, and make wind energy a key component of the global clean energy transition.Overall, the session served as a platform for knowledge exchange, fostering a collective commitment towards a sustainable and efficient future powered by wind energy.
