TALK ON CHATBOT COMBAT

Event Report

Highlights of the event/ Report of the event :
As part of the Pre-conclave event, the College of Engineering Karunagappally organized an exciting in-college event as part of the IHRD Conclave, where teams of students were challenged to create fully functioning chatbots for the main website of IHRD. The event brought together talented students from various engineering courses who had the submission date Until September 22nd to design and develop intelligent chatbots.Prof. Haseena P Y of EEE Dept, Prof Sabeena K and Jyothi R L of CS Dept evaluated Chatbots Presented by the participants. All of the participants used various AI platforms for the creation of their ChatBots and every team stood out in their own creative way . The winning team was selected on the basis of functionality and UI design of the Bot. The competition aimed to promote innovation and technical excellence, encouraging participants to craft chatbots that could enhance user experiences and engagement on the IHRD website.

CONCLUSION :
In conclusion, the Chatbot Creation Competition held at the College of Engineering Karunagappally as part of the IHRD Conclave was a remarkable event that showcased the ingenuity and technical prowess of participating students. This competition not only encouraged innovation and technical excellence but also highlighted the importance of leveraging AI and chatbot technology to enhance user experiences on websites. The dedication and creativity exhibited by the participants underscored the potential for such events to drive advancements in technology and user engagement. We commend all participants, judges, and organizers for their contributions to this successful event, which undoubtedly left a positive impact on the future of technology and innovation in education.
